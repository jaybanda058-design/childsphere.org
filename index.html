About Childsphere

A movement born from children, for children.

Childsphere is a youth-led organisation rooted in Zambia with global reach. We believe every child — regardless of where they were born — deserves to know their rights, feel safe, and have the chance to thrive.

Founded by young people who experienced the gap between rights on paper and rights in practice, we use education, creativity, emergency response and community organising to close that gap.

Rights-based approach
Child-centred design
Community-grown solutions
